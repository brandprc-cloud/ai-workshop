A

Quiz Bot for Client Qualification

Client arrives — bot asks questions, qualifies, passes only targeted leads to you. Save manager time, don't lose leads.

B

Landing Page — Understanding How It Works

Not "make a landing page", but understand what's behind it, what it actually costs, where contractors overcharge. Plus — build a working site in one day.

C

WhatsApp Automation

Broadcast to database → automatic qualification of responses. Interested leads enter the funnel. Others don't take up team time.

D

Chat and Channel Parser

Monitor competitors, industry channels, mentions. AI collects and structures — you get a digest, not a flood of raw data.

Meetings → Tasks

Meeting recording (Zoom, Google Meet) → transcript → Claude extracts tasks → automatically sent to team chat. Meetings no longer get lost.

Automatic congratulations, post-meeting follow-ups, renewal reminders. AI works with your database — you never forget clients who are "almost ready".

G

Auto-responder for DMs and Comments

AI answers typical questions in Instagram, Telegram, handles objections, passes hot leads to the manager. Without robotic bots that irritate everyone.

H

Website Parser

Monitor competitor prices, analyze market offers. You know what's happening — before others notice.
